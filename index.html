In Sharky the Brave, you play as Sharky, a heroic little shark fighting to free the ocean from evil pufferfish, jellyfish, and the deadly final boss, the killer whale. Collect coins to restore health and gather poison bottles to shoot powerful toxic bubbles. Use Sharky's tail slap to push enemies away or normal bubbles to attack. Save the reef!
